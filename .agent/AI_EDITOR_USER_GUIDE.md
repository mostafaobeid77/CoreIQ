# 🤖 AI Plan Editor - Quick Reference Guide

## How to Use

### 1. Open AI Assistant
In the Plans screen → Tap the **✨ AI button** → AI Edit Modal opens

### 2. Enter Your Command
Type naturally! Examples:

#### 🍗 Food Operations
```
"Swap all chicken for salmon"
"Add a protein shake to breakfast"
"Remove rice from dinner"
"Add 200g of eggs to lunch"
"Make my meals vegetarian"
"Add more protein"
```

#### 💪 Workout Operations  
```
"Add bench press to my workout"
"Replace squats with leg press"
"Remove cardio"
"Add pull-ups"
"Swap deadlifts for Romanian deadlifts"
```

#### 📊 Macro Adjustments
```
"Fix my protein deficit"
"Reduce calories"
"Add more carbs"
"Balance my macros"
```

### 3. AI Processes & Applies
- Understands intent
- Finds best matches
- Modifies your plan
- Shows what changed

### 4. Review Changes
You'll see a summary like:
```
✅ Added Salmon (150g) to lunch. 
High protein alternative that meets your targets.
```

## Command Templates

### Pattern: ADD
`"Add [food/exercise] to [meal/workout]"`
- Add chicken to lunch
- Add protein shake to snacks

### Pattern: SWAP/REPLACE
`"Swap/Replace [old item] with/for [new item]"`
- Swap chicken for turkey
- Replace bench press with dumbbells

### Pattern: REMOVE/DELETE
`"Remove/Delete [item] from [meal/workout]"`
- Remove bread from breakfast
- Delete cardio

### Pattern: ADJUST
`"Fix/Boost/Reduce [macro/aspect]"`
- Fix protein deficit
- Boost calories
- Reduce carbs

## Quick Tips

✅ **Be Specific**: "Add chicken to lunch" beats "Add chicken"
✅ **Use Names**: The AI knows your foods/workouts
✅ **Natural Language**: Talk normally, AI understands
✅ **One Change**: One command at a time works best
✅ **Check Results**: Always review what changed

## Example Conversations

**User**: "Add more protein to lunch"
**AI**: ✅ Added Chicken Breast (120g) to boost protein. This brings you closer to your 180g target.

**User**: "Swap pasta for rice"
**AI**: ✅ Removed Pasta from dinner. Added Brown Rice (150g) to dinner. Similar carbs with better fiber.

**User**: "Make breakfast high protein"
**AI**: ✅ Added Greek Yogurt (200g) to breakfast. Added Scrambled Eggs (100g) to breakfast. Protein boosted by 35g.

**User**: "Add deadlifts"
**AI**: ✅ Added Deadlifts to your workout. Great compound movement for back and legs.

## Troubleshooting

### "No changes made"
- Be more specific about meal type
- Check if item exists in database
- Try alternative wording

### "Couldn't find similar foods"
- Use generic names (chicken vs chicken breast)
- Try adding manually
- Check food database

### AI not responding
- System falls back to keyword matching
- Basic operations still work
- Check network connection

## Advanced Usage

### Multi-Step Edits
For complex changes:
1. First edit: "Add protein to lunch"
2. Second edit: "Remove bread from breakfast"
3. Third edit: "Add vegetables to dinner"

### Context-Aware
The AI remembers:
- Your macro targets
- Current meals/workouts
- Your fitness goal
- Available foods

So commands like "add more protein" automatically calculate the right amount!
